The Three Paradigms of AI Scaling

AI scales in discrete jumps. Build for the end state rather than the current phase. This section outlines the evolution of artificial intelligence capabilities.

◉

Predictive AI

Recognizes established patterns. Optimizes existing workflows through data forecasting. Human limits still gate its overall execution.

◎

Generative AI

Creates novel patterns. Drops the marginal cost of content creation to zero. Operates as a read-only oracle requiring human prompting.

◕

Agentic AI

Executes complex patterns. Acts as a read-write digital worker. Reasons, uses software tools, and achieves goals autonomously.

Moats & Defensibility

Capital requirements exceed $50 billion annually, allowing incumbents to make substitution economically impossible for fabless designers. ASML holds exclusive patents on extreme ultraviolet technology. Competitors cannot replicate this physical supply chain quickly or cheaply.

Vulnerabilities & Bottlenecks

Geopolitical concentration in Taiwan creates a single point of global failure. Natural disasters constantly threaten these highly sensitive fabrication plants.

Strategic Imperative Example

TSMC: Maintain a structural lead in advanced nodes and CoWoS packaging to secure monopoly margins.
