Semiconductor Manufacturing

The Atomic Constraint. Prints silicon blueprints using extreme lithography.

~15%

▲ Revenue Drivers

  • • Processed silicon wafer sales
  • • Advanced node pricing premiums

▼ Cost Drivers

  • • Multi-billion dollar fab construction
  • • $300M+ EUV lithography machines
  • • Sub-nanometer node R&D

Moats & Defensibility

Capital requirements exceed $50 billion annually, allowing incumbents to make substitution economically impossible for fabless designers. ASML holds exclusive patents on extreme ultraviolet technology. Competitors cannot replicate this physical supply chain quickly or cheaply.

Vulnerabilities & Bottlenecks

Geopolitical concentration in Taiwan creates a single point of global failure. Natural disasters constantly threaten these highly sensitive fabrication plants.

Strategic Imperative Example

TSMC: Maintain a structural lead in advanced nodes and CoWoS packaging to secure monopoly margins.
